Chocolate Cake Recipe

Home Recipes Contact

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa powder.
  3. Add eggs and milk.
  4. Stir until smooth.
  5. Pour into baking pan.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes.
