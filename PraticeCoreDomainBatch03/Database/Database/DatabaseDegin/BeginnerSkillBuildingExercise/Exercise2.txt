StudentID (PK)

StudentName

Email

Course

Primary Key: CourseCode

Attributes:

CourseCode (PK)

Title

Instructor

Enrollment (Linking Entity)

Primary Key: EnrollmentID

Attributes:

EnrollmentID (PK)

StudentID (FK)

CourseCode (FK)

2️⃣ Relationship

Relationship Name:

Student enrolls in Course

Because:

One student → many courses

One course → many students

So relationship = Many-to-Many (M:N)

Isliye Enrollment table use hoti hai.
