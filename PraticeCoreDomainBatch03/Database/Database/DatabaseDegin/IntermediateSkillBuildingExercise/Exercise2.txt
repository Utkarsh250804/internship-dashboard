2️⃣ Relationship

Relationship Name:

Author writes Book

Explanation:

One author can write multiple books

One book can have multiple authors

Therefore relationship = Many-to-Many (M:N)

Is relationship ko represent karne ke liye junction / associative table use hoti hai.
